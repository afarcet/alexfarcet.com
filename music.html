The Anti-Algorithm Jukebox

Music used to be discovered differently. Flipping through a friend's record collection. Someone handing you a CD in a parking lot. A melody drifting through a wall in a city you'd never been to. Not an algorithm feeding you more of what you already like, shrinking your world one recommendation at a time.

This is a jukebox of one person's actual taste. No collaborative filtering. No engagement optimization. Just 128 songs and the beautiful chaos of chance. Pull the lever and see what comes up.
